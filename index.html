Iveagh & Row

Derived From Nature - Defined By Hand

Iveagh in its Gaelic form refers to the hidden gardens tucked away in the heart of Dublin, Ireland. The term for Iveagh speaks to the organic systems found in nature while Row is in essence a street scape in all its complexity which speaks to the manmade hierarchies and more uniform and structured design qualities (elements). Iveagh and Row is truly a melding of the two – not man vs. nature but how the two meet and work together to create a unique experience – not quite of man but not quite of nature. The work defines a unique and new space that hovers between the organic and the defined – an unknown realm not defined completely by either.
